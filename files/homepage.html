Adoption Process >

Create an account Fill out inquiry Choose a pet to adopt Online interview Pick up your pet

Start by creating an account on our website. This will allow you to save your progress, track your applications, and receive updates.

Click on the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill out the registration form with your name, email address, and create a secure password. Verify your email to activate your account.

Having an account enables you to log in at any time to continue your adoption journey and manage your profile details.

Once your account is set up, fill out the adoption inquiry form. This form helps us understand your preferences and match you with suitable pets.

The adoption inquiry form asks about your living situation, family members, existing pets, and your preferences for a new pet (e.g., species, breed, age).

Provide detailed information to help us ensure a successful match between you and your future pet.

Browse through our gallery of adorable pets looking for a home. Select the one that tugs at your heartstrings and fits your lifestyle.

Each pet profile includes photos, a description of their personality, health status, and any special needs or requirements.

Consider factors such as the pet's temperament, energy level, and compatibility with your household to make an informed decision.

We'll schedule an online interview to discuss your application and ensure that the pet you've chosen is a good fit for your home and family.

The interview may cover topics such as your daily routine, pet care experience, and how you plan to integrate the new pet into your home environment.

It's an opportunity for us to address any questions you may have and provide advice on pet care and adjustment.

After a successful interview, you can arrange a time to pick up your new furry friend. We'll provide all the information you need to ensure a smooth transition.

Prepare your home for the new pet by setting up a designated space with food, water, bedding, and toys.

Our team will guide you on pet care basics, including feeding schedules, exercise needs, and veterinary care recommendations.

What is AGJS Pet Adoption?

Our Mission Our Vision

AGJS aims to provide support to animal welfare organizations that are continuously making an effort to provide homes to stray animals. By implementing a website that will help to increase

the adoption rate and animal awareness. Our primary goal is to make
the process of finding pets more accessible and efficient, ensuring that more animals will find loving fur parents and comfortable homes.

We envision a world where pets are provided with care and comfortable shelter. Animal welfare organizations have enough budget and resources to connect the animals with their potential fur parents.
With our website, we strive to create a community where animal welfare is one of the priorities.
